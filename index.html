  • About the Book

  • This book of tongue-in-cheek advice, inspired by the famous Russian children’s book ‘Bad Advice’ by Grigoriy Oster, is dedicated to young entrepreneurs everywhere. At the height of the political tensions in the world, it offers a universal story of struggle for success at any cost - and what it can lead to.

  • The story will take you on a globe-trotting, adrenaline-fueled adventure from Moscow to San Francisco and will offer genuine, if ironic, insight into dos and don’ts of entrepreneurship that every aspiring billionaire should know by heart.

  • We hope that through this book you, the reader, will be inspired to be entrepreneurial in every aspect of your life, and that you will also find a bit of lighthearted fun on the way there.

  • Мы надеемся, что вы получите удовольствие от чтения этой книги! Enjoy!
